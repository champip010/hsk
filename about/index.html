A Primary 6 student from Singapore

Hi there! I built this app to help me study Chinese vocabulary for school. Learning Chinese can be tough, but with flashcards and quizzes, it becomes a lot more fun!

This app supports HSK levels 1-5 and 7-9. You can import your own word lists from Excel, practice with flashcards, and test yourself with quizzes.
